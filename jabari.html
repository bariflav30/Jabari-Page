Meet The HTML Scientist


Hello, my name is Jabari Augustin, and I would like to welcome you to my portfolio page. I'm a graduate of Andrews University with a Bachelor's Degree in Graphic Imaging Technology, I've been working in the Web Development field since 2007 as Graphic/Web Designer and I shifted my focus in becoming a Front End Web Developer. Lately, I've been diving deeper in studying more about HTML 5 and CSS/CSS3 while putting it into practice, as well as learning the use of JavaScript and JQuery to enhance your website.


Being a Front End Developer is my life. It's my five-star hotel. It's my bread and butter, when I wake up in the morning. It's something that I dream about when I go to sleep at night and then wake up and turn it into a reality. Without Web Development my world would be gray. I can tell about the struggles that I went through just to get to this spot in life, but instead I rather let you know to always follow your dreams and turn it into goals. Don't doubt yourself, I believe that in order to get where you want to be in life, you got to have more faith and less fear.

I just really love sports, I'm a huge Patriots, Lions, and Celtic Fan. Other than that, I'm a pretty laid back guy that just enjoys life and embrace whatever it throws at me.
